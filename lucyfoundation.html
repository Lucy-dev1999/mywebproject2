WELCOME TO LUCY FOUNDATION

LUCY FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization dedicated to the mission of providing education, financial assistance, and social support to the less fortunate children and youths in our community. Our mission is to create a sense of belonging and pride in the children and youths, to foster a sense of community and mutual support among the community, and to create a sense of hope and resilience among the children and the community. We believe that by helping our children and youths learn, grow, and thrive, we can create a brighter, more prosperous future for ourselves and for all of our children and youths. GOALS

  • To provide education to the less fortunate children.
  • To improve the quality of life for families in vulnerable areas.
  • To provide financial assistance and support to individuals and families facing financial difficulties.
  • To promote social and economic development among the community.
  • To create a sense of belonging and pride in the children and youths.
  • To foster a sense of community and mutual support among the community.
  • To create a sense of hope and resilience among the children and the community.
  • To create a sense of empowerment and purpose among the community members.

MISION.

Bring contageous joy and hope to children, youths and the community at large.

VISION.

To create a community where every child, every family, and every individual feels safe, loved, and valued.

CORE VALUES.

  • Fear of God: To live in a way that is filled with fear and uncertainty of God's presence.
  • Love: To live in a way that fosters love, compassion, and understanding between all members of the community.
  • Compassion: To treat all members of the community with kindness and understanding.
  • Integrity: To uphold the values and principles of the foundation.
